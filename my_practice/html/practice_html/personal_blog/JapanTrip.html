Attractions

Japan is known for its cherry blossoms, castles, samurai swords, and many shrines and religious sites. Some popular attractions include Mount Fuji, which is important in Shintoism and Buddhism, and the Arashiyama Bamboo Grove in Kyoto, which is a famous sight. You can buy tickets to popular attractions in advance to avoid long lines.

Travel Preparation

You can prepare for your trip by checking visa requirements, purchasing travel insurance, choosing a season, and deciding how long you want to stay. You can also learn some basic Japanese, bring some Japanese yen, and have an itinerary in mind.
